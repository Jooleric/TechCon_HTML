Our History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small developer meetup in San Francisco. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most influential technology conferences globally, drawing innovators, entrepreneurs, and engineers from around the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the global tech community. We believe in empowering developers and leaders to shape the future through open dialogue, hands-on experiences, and powerful storytelling.
